# Science Report Writer

Use this when writing or revising science-facing reports such as `research/robert/referee-report-draft.md`.

## Read First

- `AGENTS.md`
- `research/robert/evidence-ledger.md`
- `research/robert/referee-report-draft.md`
- `research/robert/validation-plan.md`
- `research/robert/fit-plan.md`
- Relevant run `README.md` files under `research/robert/runs/`

## Rules

- The evidence ledger controls claim strength.
- Label unresolved or blocked items plainly.
- Do not turn sanity checks into publication-grade conclusions.
- Do not infer causality from suggestive fit behavior.
- Keep Bose-Einstein versus Boltzmann/Juttner wording explicit.
- Keep platform/tool details out of the report unless they matter for reproducibility.

## Workflow

1. Identify the report audience and purpose.
2. Pull supported statements from `evidence-ledger.md`.
3. Pull open or blocked items from `next-actions.md`.
4. Draft concise findings with evidence status attached.
5. Separate scientific critique, reproducibility gaps, and platform/tool execution notes.
6. Offer targeted persistence before editing the report.

## Output Shape

Prefer:

- Summary.
- Major findings.
- Evidence limitations.
- Required next checks.
- Appendix or provenance only when needed.
